Frequently Asked Questions

What products should I sell?

Sosh users loves products from brands, that fall into a variety of categories like makeup, apparel, gadgets, electronics, shoes, books, and the list goes on. Please note that Sosh is currently only available in Canada.

What is the pricing for running the products?

During our BETA period, your store functions for completely free. Once Sosh leaves the beta stages and gets released publicly, we will send you an alert. The price will likely be based off of every 1,000 views, you pay x amount.

How do I set up my shop?

To join Sosh Sellers, press the “Become a Seller” button above. We put straight-forward instructions that will guide you through setting up your store, and you can have your products running by today!

What is the “BETA” period?

When we say the “BETA” period, we mean the early access timeframe. Currently our app is exclusive to hand-picked testers which help us make Sosh even more efficient and lovable. The BETA timeframe is a great time for brands to familiarize themselves with this platform to get a huge early advantage!




1. Release products free on Sosh during the BETA stages. Once Sosh becomes public, prices will likely change to a CPM basis. Please note that prices are subject to change.
